My Hobbies
Volunteering
Being a part of NSS and VSO has shaped my sense of empathy and responsibility. I’ve actively participated in blood donation drives, campus clean-ups, and educational outreach programs. These experiences have not only made me a better team player but also taught me how to design with compassion and purpose.
Swimming
Swimming is my ultimate escape. The feeling of gliding through the water, weightless and free, is truly unparalleled. Whether it's in the calm waters of a pool or the waves of the open sea, swimming rejuvenates my body and soul.
Social Hangouts
Spending quality time with friends is a treasured escape from the routine. Whether it's sharing laughter, exploring new places, or simply enjoying each other's company, social hangouts create lasting bonds and cherished memories.
Painting
I find peace and expression through sketching and painting. It’s my creative outlet where I experiment with colors, characters, and moods. Whether it’s detailed pencil sketches or playful watercolor pieces, art helps me observe the world with a sharper eye — a skill I carry into my design work.
