Applications Analysed
Cleanshot impressed me with how clean the interface looks, even though it has a lot of features. I tried to figure out how they kept it simple — through smart icon use, clean layouts, and little details like hover animations. It taught me that good UI doesn’t have to be loud to be powerful.
I’ve always loved how clean and simple Notion feels. While exploring it, I focused on how they use whitespace, clear typography, and a modular layout to keep everything organized without looking messy. It made me realize how powerful minimalist design can be when done right.
Headspace felt calming the moment I opened it, and that really stuck with me. I paid attention to their soft colors, friendly illustrations, and how smooth the onboarding was. It helped me understand how design can create an emotional experience, not just a functional one.
